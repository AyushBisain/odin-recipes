Lasagna Recipe

Serving of a piping hot lasagna

Great meal to prepare for dinner.

Be vigilant of any lactose intolerant folks as the recipe uses a lot of cheese.

Ingredients

  • Cheese
  • Minced Beef
  • Vegetables

Instructions

  1. Prepare many slices of cheese
  2. Inbetween the slices spread minced beef and vegetables
  3. after about 30-35 layers of cheese, vegetables and minced beef have formed prepare to put the tray into the oven
  4. Leave at 80 degree celsius for about 45 minutes
